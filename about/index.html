Blank Aberration is an audio/visual duo based in Brooklyn, NYC.

Blank Aberration began as a collaboration in contemporary classical composition that quickly stretched into interdisciplinary performance. Combining traditional composition with experimental electronic media, Adam Lutz (music & sound design) and Kristian de Leon (projections/stage design) construct immersive environments of sound and light, engaging a dynamic and richly textural style that they call "ambient brutalism."

Their upcoming project, WINNOWS, utilizes modular synthesis processing live percussion through a multi-speaker array, and features dynamic lighting and generative visual projections. www.adamlutzmusic.com
